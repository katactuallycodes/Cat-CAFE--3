Welcome to Whiskers & Lattes

Established in 2023, Whiskers & Lattes is San Francisco's premier cat cafe where you can enjoy delicious coffee and treats while surrounded by adorable feline friends.

Our mission is to find forever homes for rescue cats while providing a unique and relaxing experience for our human guests.

Cat Adoption

All our cats are available for adoption through our partnership with local shelters.

Quality Coffee

We serve locally roasted, ethically sourced coffee and homemade pastries.

Community

We host events and workshops focused on animal welfare and cat care.
